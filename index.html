Hello and Welcome to my personal webpage! My name is Jess and I am a junior Computer Science major at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County. Prior to this new and exciting chapter, I was a chemical engineer and these 2 quotes can sum up my decision to switch fields: "Data is the new oil" and "I hope you live a life you're proud of. If you find that you're not, I hope you have the courage to start all over again." by F. Scott Fitzgerald.

Computer Science has taught me what I have found to be the most important directions in this journey called life: 1) you learn by failing, multiple times my code didnt work 2) you become who you surround yourself around, I aspire to be someone who can inspire others to believe in themselves and 3) never stop learning.
